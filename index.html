Introduction

Monitoring climate changes using IoT technology is an emerging trend that has the potential to change the world. The project aims to monitor the soil quality to solve the problems that may face plant growth. Soil temperature and soil moisture are the basic parameters that help in characterizing the soil quality. That will help in taking proper decisions regarding fertilizer application and choice of crops sown. They provide a mechanistic link between climate and growing conditions. In this work, a temperature sensor (MAX6675 K-type) is used for temperature measurements. For soil moisture content estimation, the inverse relation between soil resistance and soil moisture has been utilized. The system is integrated with ESP for the transfer of data to the website.
